About Us

Our Story

Let's Meat Here Shisanyama is a popular establishment located in Sunninghill, Johannesburg, at the intersection of Kikuyu Road and Leeuwkop Road. Founded in 2018, it has grown into a well-loved spot for those who enjoy delicious grilled meats, gourmet dishes, and a wide selection of refreshing drinks.

With its inviting atmosphere and commitment to quality, it has become a go-to destination for social gatherings, casual dining, and special occasions, attracting both locals and visitors looking for a great culinary experience.

Our Values

  • Community: Let's meat here Shisanyama, where people gather to enjoy mouthwatering food and pleasant company, is a symbol of community and togetherness.
  • Tradition: Deeply ingrained in South African customs and culture, shisanyama frequently celebrates local food and cooking methods.
  • Quality: Shisanyama places a strong importance on using fresh ingredients and premium cuts of meat to produce mouthwatering dishes that customers will love.
  • Hospitality:We place significant value on offering outstanding customer service and making every customer feel welcome.
  • Innovation:Shisanyama promotes creativity and innovation in creating new and fascinating foods for customers to enjoy, even if traditional flavors and cooking techniques are still crucial.
